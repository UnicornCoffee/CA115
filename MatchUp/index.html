What can MatchUp do for you?

Our Services:

MatchUp is a player first application which aims to bring people together over a shared love of football.
With our successful partnerships with pitches nationwide, combined with our game finder and team making tools, MatchUp is the only tool needed to enjoy frequent 5-a-side matches.

Our users get a 10% discount code to use at our affilated partners towards pitch rental. This offer is applied on a second rental, automatically upon purchasing their first.

Using personal ELO-ratings, our matching technology ensures that players will be matched with others of a similar skill level.
These ratings are also applied to our leaderboards, where users can win monthly prizes.

We've partnered with 40 Premier League football players to give users a chance to meet and train with them. Users will automatically be entered into our monthly draw after entering a competitive pool.
